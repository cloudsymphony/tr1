TR-1 privacy policy

Effective 2026-07-05

The short version

TR-1 stores your data on your device. It has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party SDKs. Nothing you do in the app is transmitted to the developer or anyone else.

What the app stores, and where

  • Your training data — program, workouts, sets, weights, notes — is stored locally on your iPhone. It is included in your normal iPhone backup like any other app's data.
  • Apple Health: with your permission, TR-1 reads workouts, body weight, resting heart rate, sleep, and steps from Apple Health, and writes finished strength workouts and manual body-weight entries back to it. You control this access in Settings ▸ Health, and the app works (with reduced features) if you decline.
  • Weather: on run days the app requests a forecast from Apple's WeatherKit using an approximate (rounded) location. Location is never stored or shared.
  • Export: you can export your data as JSON/CSV files at any time from within the app; those files go only where you choose to save or share them.

What the app does not do

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind.
  • No data sent to the developer or any third party.
  • No account, sign-in, or cloud database.

Beta (TestFlight)

TR-1 is distributed through Apple's TestFlight. If you choose to send feedback or crash reports through TestFlight, Apple shares that feedback (including any screenshots you attach) with the developer under Apple's privacy terms.

Contact

Questions or requests: use the Send Feedback option in TestFlight.
