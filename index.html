Trainer / Tracker · Unit One

TR-1BETA

A hybrid strength + running coach for iPhone. It prescribes today's session, progresses your lifts automatically, builds your running volume week over week, and pulls the rest of the picture — runs, weight, sleep, resting heart rate, steps — from Apple Health.

Getting in

  1. Install TestFlight from the App Store.
  2. Open your invite link (sent to you directly) and accept.
  3. Install TR-1 from TestFlight.
  4. On first launch, pick Use starter program (a ready-made week: three lift days, two runs, an 18-week running build) or Start empty and build your own in the Program tab. Everything is editable later either way.
  5. Grant Apple Health access when asked — that's how your runs, weight, sleep, and heart rate flow in, and how finished workouts count toward your Apple Fitness rings.

First-run choice

What it does

Today

One screen tells you what's owed today — lift, run, or rest — with weather-aware run windows on run days.

Guided sessions

Step-through sets with an automatic rest timer and chime. Weights progress by double progression: hit the top of the rep range, the load moves up.

Running ramp

Easy + long runs build gradually over 18 weeks, with a Zone-2 heart-rate band computed from your own Health data.

Travel modes

No equipment? Hotel gym? Pull-up bar only? Each lift day has circuit variants — and a two-minute Minimum floor for the worst days.

Health, both ways

Watch runs, weight, sleep, resting HR, and steps import automatically; finished lifts write back to Apple Fitness.

Yours to change

The whole program is editable in-app — days, exercises, sets, reps, the run ramp. There's a Home-Screen widget too.

Good to know

  • Your data lives on your phone (and in Apple Health, for what you share). Details: privacy policy.
  • Back up anytime from Settings ▸ Export / Import — deleting the app deletes its data.
  • Found a bug? Take a screenshot inside the app and TestFlight will offer to send it as feedback — that goes straight to the developer.
  • Units default to imperial; switch to metric in Settings.
